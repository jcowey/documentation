      • parseForSearchType

        public StringSearchFacet.SearchType parseForSearchType()
        Determines the SearchType of a clause, depending on the search target, clause roles, and characteristics of the search-string itself. The rules governing the determination of search-type are: (i) Searches on metadata and translation fields are SearchType.PHRASE searches (ii) Lemma, Regex, and Proximity searches are all distinct types of search (iii) Quote-delimited strings are SearchType.PHRASE searches (iv) In the absence of the above, the default search-type is SearchType.SUBSTRING
