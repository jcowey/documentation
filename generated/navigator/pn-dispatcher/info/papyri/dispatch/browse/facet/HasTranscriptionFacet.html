public class HasTranscriptionFacet
extends BooleanFacet

Facet regarding whether or not a transcription is associated with a record. Note that, although this Facet is logically and in appearance a BooeleanFacet, the transcription field is not in fact a Boolean one. This Facet accordingly tests whether or not the field has a value associated with it at all, treating absence of a value as 'false' and presence as 'true'. It might thus be termed a 'pseudo-Boolean'.
