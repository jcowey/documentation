fully_qualified_and_simple?(xpath)

checks whether xpath is fully qualified from root to tip and whether it is free of functional logic and pattern matching sth. like would be ok: /abc/a[@a=‘a’]/b[@b=‘b’]/c[@c1=‘c1’][@xml:c2=‘c2’]/@c3
